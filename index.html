Leandro Locsin

Cape Dutch

Inspired by the historic Cape Dutch style, known for its distinctive gable design and unique blend of European and African influences. The project features a collection of elegant, whitewashed rooms with thatched roofs, set against a breathtaking natural backdrop of rolling hills and vineyards. The architecture seamlessly combines traditional elements with modern amenities, creating a harmonious balance between old-world charm and contemporary luxury.
